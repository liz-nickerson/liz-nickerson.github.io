About me

Born and raised in a Cape Cod marsh, I now find myself a second semester senior at the University of Massachusetts-Amherst, expecting to graduate in May 2017. Upon graduation I will receive my Bachelor of Arts in English with a letter of specialization in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. After graduation, I plan to pursue a career in technical writing, with a focus in user experience and design.

I'm fascinated by the intersection of art and technology, and I'm excited to work in a field that values both. As an end user advocate and an information enthusiast, I strive to create usable documentation that helps people achieve their goals. In my spare time I enjoy walking through the woods, playing with dogs, and taking high-resolution photos of plants.
