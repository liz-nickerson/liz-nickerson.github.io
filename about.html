About

In May 2017, I graduated from the University of Massachusetts Amherst with a Bachelor of Arts in English and a specialization in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. One year prior, I began to develop my professional skills, while completing a year-long internship at Oracle. Currently, I'm employed as a technical writer at Dimensional Insight, where I produce online help and print deliverables.

I'm fascinated by the intersection of art and technology, and I'm thankful that I get to work in a field that values both. As an end user advocate and an information enthusiast, I strive to create usable documentation that helps people achieve their goals.

In my spare time, I enjoy walking through the woods, playing with dogs, and taking high-resolution photos of plants.
