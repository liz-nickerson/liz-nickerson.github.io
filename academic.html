A Proposal, On Behalf of the Orleans Historical Society

A mock grant proposal that I wrote for my Introduction to Professional Writing course at UMass. My grant proposal requests funding from the National Park Service to restore the CG36500 motor lifeboat, which was featured in Disney's The Finest Hours.

High Quality Problems from Jon Scieszka's Math Curse

An analytical essay that explores the cognitive value of a piece of children's literature. Originally, I came to UMass intending to become an elementary school teacher. This document is an artifact from my methods for teaching mathematics course.

O Captain America! My Captain America!

An essay for my comparative literature class, in which I compare Captain America and Abraham Lincoln. One of my more whimsical assignments, this essay was published in the 2017 edition of Writing the World: An Anthology of Writing in Comparative Literature.
